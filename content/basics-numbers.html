# Numbers & Currency Formatting of numbers and currency is especially important for Bionic applications. Media Plans are all about the numbers: how much does a placement cost? how many impressions do you want to serve? what is the Net media rate for a placement. ## Numbers Use commas for numbers over 999 99,999,999 ## Currency Separate currency values from currency symbols with a space $ 123,999.00 Currency symbol should be in medium gray ## Alignment Align numbers to the **right** when they are displayed in a tabular format Align currency symbols to the left (accounting formatting)

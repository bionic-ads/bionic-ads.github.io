# Grid with Filters and Actions ## General Pattern The basic approach for this layout is to combine three main components as the foundation for a range of features: - [Filters](./#/components-filters) - what data you see - [Action Menus](./#/components-action-menus) - what you can do to that data - [Grid](./#/components-grids) - data you want to view or take action on ![Grid with Filters and Actions](images/content/grid-with-filters-and-actions.png)
